About Jawahar Bal Manch

Who We Are

Jawahar Bal Manch is a children's movement inspired by the ideals of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. It encourages leadership, creativity, patriotism and social responsibility among children.

Our Mission

To empower children through educational, cultural and social activities while developing responsible future citizens.

Our Vision

To build a generation of confident, compassionate and socially responsible young leaders.

Relevance in the Present Context

In today's fast-changing world, nurturing socially conscious and well-rounded young individuals is essential. Jawahar Bal Manch helps build resilient youth prepared to contribute positively to society.

Future Vision

The Manch envisions expanding across Kerala, embracing digital platforms, launching scholarship programmes and partnering with educational and cultural institutions.

Our Inspiration

"The children of today will make the India of tomorrow."
— Jawaharlal Nehru
