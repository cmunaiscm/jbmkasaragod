About Jawahar Bal Manch

Jawahar Bal Manch is a national children's movement inspired by the ideals of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. We aim to develop leadership, creativity, patriotism and social responsibility among children.

Our Mission

  • Develop Leadership
  • Promote Creativity
  • Build National Unity
  • Serve Society
